About Us

This project is developed as a major academic undertaking by a group of four students. We are collaborating to simplify information extraction from YouTube videos.

We believe in the power of technology to make our lives easier and more efficient. Our goal is to provide users with a tool that allows them to quickly summarize YouTube videos and access the most important information without wasting time.
